Sign up

There are four sections to the signup procedure.
1. To begin, fill in your basic information.
2. In the usertype, you’re supposed to choose the “USER” option.
3. Click the “NEXT” button once you’ve filled out all the fields.
NOTE: You will only be able to register if you are over the age of 65.

Schedule Meeting

1. To schedule a meeting, start by selecting the type of user you want to meet with: Volunteer or Elder.
2. Depending on the option you choose, you'll be shown with a list of nearby users.

3. Choose the time and date of the meeting. Also, give a brief reason for the meeting.

NOTE: When you schedule a meeting with a volunteer, they will be notified, and the event will appear in the “Unconfirmed Events” section. Only if the volunteer is available at the provided time will the meet be confirmed, and you will be able to view the event in the “Confirmed Events” tab.

Events

1. In the events section, there are two tabs - Confirmed Events and Unconfirmed Events.
2. The Unconfirmed Events tab will display all the events that have been scheduled with the volunteer but have not yet been confirmed.
3. The Confirmed Events tab will display all of the confirmed events.

4. After the agreed-upon meeting time, a "Did y'all meet:" section will be provided for the meetings with the Volunteers.
5. If the meeting took place, press the ✔ button.
6. Click the ✗ button if the meeting didn't take place.
